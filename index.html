About Flourish Phoenix

A middle school rooted in Central Phoenix.

Flourish Phoenix is a new kind of school — built around the belief that learning should be joyful, rigorous, and deeply personal. We combine hands-on project-based Pursuits, personalized coaching, and a tight-knit community so that every student can genuinely thrive.
